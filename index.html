Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our courses.

Do I need prior music production experience? +

While some basic familiarity with a DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) is helpful, our course is designed to accommodate beginners. We start with fundamentals and progressively move to more advanced techniques.

What equipment do I need? +

You'll need a computer with a DAW installed (Ableton Live, FL Studio, Logic Pro, etc.), headphones, and an internet connection. A MIDI keyboard is optional but recommended.

How interactive are the courses? +

Very! We include collaborative sessions, sample pack challenges, remix exchanges, and sound design battles. You'll be actively engaged with other students and instructors throughout the course.

What's included in the VIP Producer Mentorship? +

Our premium VIP offering includes everything in the standard course plus:

  • Four private 60-minute one-on-one sessions with the instructor
  • Personalized feedback on your tracks
  • Custom production roadmap tailored to your goals
  • Priority support for technical questions
  • Lifetime access to course updates
